About PARVAH

PARVAH is our college's biggest annual five-day festival. The fest includes technical events, non-technical competitions, sports, gaming, cultural performances, fun activities, DJ Night and Celebrity Night. Students from different departments participate to showcase their talent and enjoy an unforgettable experience.
